About Me

Hello, I am Pierce. I have lived in Michigan my entire life except for 6 years when I lived in New Jersey. I moved back to Michigan to help take care of my aging parents in the summer of 2019. I have been fascinated with computers, animation, video editing and programming since High School. In my free time I love playing with my dog, hanging out with family, go biking around town and watching TV and movies. That is just a little about me.

After working in Customer Service on and off since I was in High School, I made the courageous decision to try and teach myself web design and software development. Given the situation the world has been in since the beginning of the year I have really dived into the Information Technology world. I have taken several courses from Coursera.org as well as several other self-learning websites. Now that I have had all this time on my hands this year, I decided to hit the books again. I look forward to learning new skills and putting my existing skills to the test.
